Task 15 – Vulnerability Assessment Analysis

Objective:
Perform vulnerability assessment and prioritize security risks.

Tool Used:
Nmap vulnerability scanner on Kali Linux.

Process Performed:

1. Identified the target system (localhost).
2. Performed service detection scan.
3. Conducted aggressive scan for OS and service information.
4. Executed vulnerability scripts.
5. Reviewed scan results.
6. Demonstrated CVE and CVSS mapping methodology.
7. Classified vulnerabilities based on risk level.
8. Proposed remediation strategies.

Findings:
No open ports were identified on the system. This indicates that no external services are exposed, reducing the attack surface.

Risk Analysis:
Even when vulnerabilities are not present, security assessments must include evaluation of potential threats and prioritization strategies.

Remediation Strategy:
Maintain updated software, apply security patches regularly, and follow secure system configuration practices.

Conclusion:
The system currently shows minimal exposure to network-based vulnerabilities. The assessment successfully demonstrates vulnerability identification, risk classification, and remediation planning.
